About Andy

Andy (Addon Analyzer) is an Elder Scrolls Online addon that helps protect the community by identifying malicious, stolen, or problematic addons. It monitors your installed addons and alerts you if any match our database of flagged content.

🛡️

Automatic Protection

Andy automatically scans your addons every time you load the game, checking for any new or updated problematic addons.

🔍

Manual Scanning

Run /andy scan at any time to perform a full analysis of your installed addons.

⚙️

Customizable Alerts

Control warning sounds, banners, and notification preferences with simple commands.

👥

Community Driven

Our database is maintained by the community. Help us keep ESO safe by reporting problematic addons.

Available Commands

  • /andy scan - Manually scan for flagged addons
  • /andy suppress - Suppress current warnings
  • /andy monitor - Re-enable warnings
  • /andy sound - Toggle warning sound
  • /andy announce - Toggle center screen banner
  • /andy quiet - Toggle quiet mode
  • /andy debug - Enable debug mode
