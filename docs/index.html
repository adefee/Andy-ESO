About Andy

Andy (Addon Analyzer) is an Elder Scrolls Online addon that helps protect the community by identifying malicious, stolen, paywalled, or ToS/EULA-violating addons. It monitors your installed addons and alerts you if any match our watchlist of flagged content.

🛡️

Automatic Protection

Andy automatically scans your addons every time you load the game, checking for any new or updated problematic addons.

🔍

Manual Scanning

Run /andy scan at any time to perform a full analysis of your installed addons against the watchlist.

⚙️

Customizable Alerts

Control warning sounds, banners, and notification preferences with simple commands. Suppress warnings until updates.

👥

Community Driven

Our watchlist is maintained by the community. Help us keep ESO safe by reporting problematic addons.

Available Commands

/andy scan — Manually scan for flagged addons
/andy suppress — Suppress current warnings
/andy monitor — Re-enable warnings
/andy sound — Toggle warning sound
/andy announce — Toggle center screen banner
/andy quiet — Toggle quiet mode
/andy debug — Enable debug mode
